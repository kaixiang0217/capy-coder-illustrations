# Capybara Coder IP

> A chill capybara character that loves coding and sleeping, drawn in black and white minimalist hand-drawn style.
>
> 16:9 Horizontal | Capybara IP | Pure White Background | Hand-Sketched Black Lines | Minimal Red/Orange/Blue Annotations

---

## Character Profile

### Name
**Capy Coder** (or just "Capy")

### Characteristics
- **Body**: Large, rounded capybara silhouette with organic curves
- **Eyes**: Simple black dots, often half-closed (sleepy aesthetic)
- **Vibe**: Chill, calm, content - reflects the capybara's naturally relaxed nature
- **Activities**: Coding at laptop, taking naps, keyboard shortcuts, debugging

### Core Traits
- **Loves Coding**: Always shown with a laptop, coding is life
- **Loves Sleeping**: Frequently depicted napping or in pajamas
- **Fearless**: Not stressed about bugs or deadlines (typical capybara energy)
- **Social**: Capybaras are social animals - often shown with other creatures or team members

---

## Visual Style Guidelines

### Dos ✓
- Black thin lines with subtle hand-drawn wobble
- Pure white background (no gradients, shadows, or textures)
- Large areas of empty space (40-60% content, rest is breathing room)
- Simple black dots for eyes
- Capybara's characteristic rounded, plump body shape
- Show the capybara in relatable coder/sleep poses
- Use minimal red/orange/blue annotations for emphasis
- Add a laptop/keyboard whenever possible to show coding

### Don'ts ✗
- No detailed fur texture or shading
- No gray tones - pure black on white only
- No cute/chibi style - keep it organic and slightly abstract
- No complex backgrounds or environments
- No speaking bubbles or too much text
- No making capybara look stressed or angry
- Don't hide the capybara's form in decoration

## Proportions & Anatomy

### Capybara Proportions
- **Body**: 2-2.5x head width, slightly longer than tall
- **Head**: Round, takes up about 20-25% of body height
- **Eyes**: Small, simple dots, positioned 1/3 from top of head
- **Legs**: Stubby and short (capybaras are chubby ground-dwellers)
- **Overall**: Always appears solid and grounded, never spindly

## Notes for AI Generation

When generating capybara illustrations:
1. Always draw a recognizable capybara silhouette (round, plump, distinctive snout)
2. Keep eyes simple (dots or minimal lines)
3. Ensure the laptop/coding element is clearly visible
4. Use only black lines on white background
5. Leave significant white space
6. The capybara should look inherently calm and unbothered
7. Hand-drawn wobble should be subtle, not exaggerated
8. The mood should be: chill, relatable, slightly humorous
