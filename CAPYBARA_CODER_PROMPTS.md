# Capybara Coder Illustration Prompts

This file contains ready-to-use prompts for generating Capybara Coder illustrations in the Ian Xiaohei style.

---

## Core Style Parameters (Use for ALL prompts)

**Visual Style**:
- Black and white hand-drawn line art
- Pure white background, absolutely no gradients or textures
- Black ink lines only, thin and delicate with subtle wobble
- Minimalist aesthetic with 40-60% content and large white space
- No shading, no gray tones, pure black on white only
- Organic, slightly imperfect hand-sketched appearance

**Character**: Large, round capybara with simple black dot eyes, calm expression, must be clearly recognizable as a capybara

---

## Prompt 1: Debugging Mode

### Description
A chill capybara sitting at a laptop, peacefully debugging while lines of tangled code swirl around in the background. The capybara looks completely unbothered and calm despite the chaos.

### Full Prompt
```
Create a black and white hand-drawn illustration in the style of Ian Xiaohei minimalist design.

Subject: A large, round capybara with simple black dot eyes, sitting at a laptop computer. The capybara has one paw on the keyboard and looks peaceful and unbothered. Around the laptop, there are tangled lines representing confused or chaotic code, but the capybara remains serene.

Style Requirements:
- Pure white background with NO gradients, shadows, or textures
- Black hand-drawn lines only, thin and delicate with subtle wobble
- Minimalist composition: capybara and laptop occupy about 50% of the frame, rest is white space
- No gray tones whatsoever - pure black on white only
- Organic, slightly imperfect hand-sketched appearance
- No shading or texture details
- Capybara must be clearly recognizable as a capybara - round body, short stubby legs, characteristic snout

Composition: 16:9 horizontal format, capybara on left/center side of frame with laptop, tangled code lines floating around but not overwhelming the space.

Mood: Calm, unbothered, slightly humorous - the capybara is clearly at peace with debugging.

Optional annotation area: Leave white space in upper right corner for potential text annotation like "这不是问题，这是特性" (This isn't a bug, it's a feature).
```

### Use Case
- Debugging/troubleshooting blog posts
- "Stay calm while coding" motivational content
- Error handling documentation

## How to Use These Prompts

### With DALL-E 3:
1. Copy the "Full Prompt" section
2. Paste into DALL-E 3
3. Adjust the "Style Requirements" section if needed
4. Generate and iterate

### With Midjourney:
1. Copy the full prompt
2. Add `/imagine` prefix
3. Paste and adjust parameters with `--ar 16:9` for aspect ratio
4. Use `--style raw` or similar for hand-drawn effect

### With Stable Diffusion:
1. Use the prompt as-is
2. Add negative prompts: "gray, shading, gradients, texture, color, realistic, 3D, cute, chibi"
3. Set image dimensions to 16:9 ratio (e.g., 1920x1080 or 1600x900)

### Within Codex/Claude Code Skills:
1. Use these prompts with Claude's image generation capabilities
2. Reference the CAPYBARA_IP.md file for character consistency
3. Save outputs to `assets/capybara-coder/`

## QA Checklist Before Saving

- [ ] Pure white background (no gradients, texture, or shadows)
- [ ] Black lines only (no gray tones or other colors except optional red/orange/blue annotations)
- [ ] Capybara is clearly recognizable as a capybara
- [ ] Hand-drawn wobble is subtle and organic, not exaggerated
- [ ] 40-60% content, 40-60% white space
- [ ] Capybara is not cute/chibi style - it's abstract and slightly quirky
- [ ] Eyes are simple dots or minimal lines
- [ ] Laptop or coding element is clearly visible (where applicable)
- [ ] Overall mood matches the scenario (calm, unbothered, relatable)
- [ ] No small details that clash with minimalist style
- [ ] No text or annotations in the generated image itself (leave room for optional text overlay)

## Tips for Better Generation

1. **If the background has texture or color**: Add to negative prompt: "no paper texture, no beige, no cream, pure white only"
2. **If the capybara doesn't look right**: Emphasize "large round capybara, NOT a guinea pig, NOT a hamster, characteristic capybara snout"
3. **If there are too many details**: Add "minimalist, simple, clean lines, remove all small details"
4. **If lines are too thick or not hand-drawn**: Specify "thin black lines, hand-drawn wobble, sketch style"
5. **If colors appear**: Specify "black and white only, absolutely no colors except black and white"
